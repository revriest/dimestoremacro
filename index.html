Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BareMacros

Does it work offline?

Yes! Your daily logs and custom saved meals are stored locally on your device, so you can track your macros anywhere. However, searching the global database for new foods is limited without an active internet connection.

Does it track total calories?

Yes, it does. While we heavily emphasize hitting your core macros (Protein, Carbs, and Fat) for body composition, BareMacros automatically calculates and tracks your total daily calories so you always have the complete picture.

How do I back up my data?

Since BareMacros is privacy-first and stores everything locally, we recommend using your device's native backup system (iCloud for iOS or Google Drive backup for Android) to ensure your data is safe if you switch devices.

Where does the food data come from?

We use a combination of high-quality regional databases and the OpenFoodFacts database as a fallback. Our data is curated to ensure accuracy for the most common foods and brands.

Do I need to create an account?

Absolutely not. BareMacros is a privacy-first, local app. You just download it, open it, and start tracking immediately. No email, no password, no BS.

Is it really free?

Yes, all of the core tracking features you need to hit your goals are 100% free and always will be, with zero ads to slow you down.
